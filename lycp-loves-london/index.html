LYCP loves restaurants

The Stockpot
18 Old Compton Street, London W1D 4TN
Traditional
Cheap, cheerful grub. Full roast, salads, pasta, scampi, jelly & ice cream is a highlight. The wine is drinkable. Old gents hunkered down near drag queens & shoulder to shoulder with tourists. Book for a large group. Cash only.
Marie’s Cafe
90 Lower Marsh, London SE1 7AB
Thai
Greasy spoon in the daytime, glorious byob thai at night.
Ping Pong
All over town
Dim Sum
Ask for the loyalty card which = free prawn crackers and dipping sauces and emailed loyalty deals.
My Old Place
88-90 Middlesex Street, London E1 7EZ
Chinese / Szechuan
Take a big group, and get lots to share. Cheap, yum and proper chinese
Loong Kee
134G Kingsland Road, London E2 8DY
Vietnamese
BYOB, cash only, the best of the Vietnamese bunch in our opinion!
Le Mercury
140a Upper Street, London N1 1QY
French
Great place for dates. There's now another branch right next door. Proper french food at reasonable prices
La Petite Auberge
283 Upper Street, London N1 2TZ
French
Great french food, over the road from Le Mercury. Just as good!
Pierre Victoire
5 Dean Street, London W1D 3RQ
French
Our fave french in soho. Piano in the corner for impromptu playing, seating upstairs is better than downstairs.
Wong Kei
Chinatown, Soho
Chinese
Very cheap, good food, a London institution that is renowned for it’s rude but comical service!
Lantana Cafe Fitzrovia
13 Charlotte Place, London W1T 1SN
Breakfast & brunch
Amazing Aussie place with a very yummy brunch menu, no booking, so get there early to avoid a long queue!
Lahore Kebab house
E1 1PY
Pakistani
A brilliant menu, the meat is to die for, plus it’s BYOB!
Dishoom
Covent Garden, Kings X and Shoreditch
Street-style indian
Authentic, cheap, Indian street food
