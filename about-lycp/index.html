About LYCP

Who are we?
London Young Charity Professionals (LYCP) is a networking group for young professionals in the charity sector in and around London.

Why are we here?
To create an informal and friendly space to get to know like-minded peers in the sector. To share ideas, challenges and to support each other.

Who can get involved?
Young charity professionals, people who are new to the sector or who are interested in working in the sector.

What do we do?
LYCP hosts a free-to-attend bimonthly drinks event called Third Thursday at a central London location for relaxed networking and socialising. We'll soon relaunch our successful CEO Speaker Series; The Inspirationals.

You can find out when and where the next bimonthly Third Thursday drinks are by looking at the 'next event' tab to the right and also joining our mailing list which will ensure you get timely reminders for upcoming drinks events.
