Regular events

LYCP hosts a free-to-attend drinks event every first Thursday of the month at a central London location, for relaxed networking and socialising.

LYCP First Thursday drinks are free to attend and don't have any set introductions, speeches or games to take part in. We don't actively encourage card swapping and 'enforced networking', leaving it up to you to share details. LYCP is more about socialising and having a chance to meet like-minded people working in and around London in a friendly and non-pressured environment. A wide variety of attendees are welcome to networking events, with no joining requirements apart from classing yourself as a London-based 'Young Charity Professional'.
